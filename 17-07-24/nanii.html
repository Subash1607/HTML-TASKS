Nani

Introduction

Nani, whose full name is Naveen Babu Ghanta, is a popular Indian actor and producer predominantly known for his work in Telugu cinema.

Early Life

Born on February 24, 1984, in Hyderabad, India, Nani initially pursued a career in software engineering before venturing into acting.

Career

He made his debut as an actor in the 2008 Telugu film Ashta Chamma, which was critically acclaimed. Since then, he has starred in numerous successful films, showcasing his versatility as an actor.

Popular Films

Some of his notable films include Eega, Bhale Bhale Magadivoy, Jersey, and Gentleman. These films have not only been commercially successful but have also earned him praise for his performances.

Awards

Nani has received several awards throughout his career, including Filmfare Awards and Nandi Awards, for his outstanding contributions to Telugu cinema.

Personal Life

Nani is known for his down-to-earth personality and is active on social media, where he interacts with his fans.
